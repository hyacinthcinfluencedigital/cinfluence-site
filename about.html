The Journey

Every chapter taught
her something different.

The Beginning

Reseller. Student. Entrepreneur.

Before she had a business name, Cinth had a hustle. She began as a reseller while still in school — learning to market products, connect with customers, and generate sales through persistence and creativity alone. That early chapter planted the seed of everything that followed.

Age 16

The Store. The Camera. The Stage.

She owned and operated her own store in the Philippines — a milestone that caught the attention of national television. Recognized not just for what she built, but for why it mattered. Alongside the store, she was already creating content: vlogging, filming, editing, building an online presence long before "content creator" was a career title. That love for storytelling and audience engagement never left.

Building

Across Industries, Never in One Lane.

Cinth immersed herself in gadgets, fashion, food, and hospitality — handling operations, branding, marketing, and sales at every level. She owned and managed a restobar, where branding lived in atmosphere and experience, not just a feed. Each industry taught her something different about how brands grow and how people decide to trust a business.

Strategy

Financial Advisor. Trust Builder.

Working as a Financial Advisor sharpened a different set of skills: communication, professionalism, client relationship management, and an understanding of consumer behavior and long-term thinking. She learned how to build trust with people who had something real at stake — and how to deliver value that outlasts a single conversation. That experience carries directly into how she works with clients today.

The Pivot

Redirected Into Digital.

When personal and business challenges mounted, Cinth didn't retreat from marketing — she redirected into it. She moved into the digital space as a Social Media Manager and Virtual Assistant, helping brands build online presence, create content with purpose, and develop real audience relationships. Freelance work became a vision. The vision became a business.

2026

Cinfluence.

Today, Cinfluence is the culmination of every role she has held, every industry she has navigated, and every lesson — successful or painful — she has carried forward. A remote digital marketing business built on strategy, creativity, authenticity, and impactful storytelling. More than a service, it represents a belief: that experience, passion, and consistency create opportunity — regardless of age, background, or how many times something didn't work the first time.
